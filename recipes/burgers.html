Best Burger Ever Recipe

Are you ready to try the best Burger ever?

Most people love burgers, most people make great burgers. However, these are the best burgers!

Ingredience

  • Burger Patties
  • Sesame-Seed Bunns
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Finely Chopped Lettus
  • Sliced Tomato Slices
  • Diced onion
  • Piccle Slices
  • Cheese Slices
  • Optional - Pepper and Sault

Steps

  1. Toast your Bunns for 2 to 3 minutes in a toaster oven or the grill.
  2. Take 1 or 2 of your burger patties and place them on the grill. Cook until it's well done(No pink) on both sides.
  3. After cooking your patties, let's start making our sause.
  4. In a bowl, squert a bit of mayo, ketchup and yellow mustard. Mix all until it's all blended together!
  5. Next, take your patties and bunns and start assembling. Put your patties on the bunns and add a bit of our sause mixture.
  6. Next add your tomato slice and your piccles to the bottom bunn.
  7. Finish things off by adding the lettus and onions to the patty below the top bunn.

Congradulations, you are done!
